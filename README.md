# Business Model Canvas Guide

A comprehensive guide for creating and understanding Business Model Canvas (BMC) with detailed writing guidelines, practical examples, and a complete sample business plan.

## 📋 Contents

This repository contains:

1. **Business_Model_Canvas_Guide.md** - Complete markdown guide with:
   - Introduction to the Business Model Canvas
   - Detailed writing guidelines for all 9 building blocks
   - Tips, examples, and common pitfalls for each section
   - Quick reference checklist
   - Complete GreenBowl sample business plan
   - Visual Mermaid diagrams

2. **Business_Model_Canvas_Guide.html** - Professional HTML version:
   - Fully rendered with Mermaid diagrams
   - Table of contents with navigation
   - Responsive design for mobile and desktop
   - Ready to view in any web browser

3. **template.html** - Pandoc HTML template:
   - Includes Mermaid diagram support
   - Professional styling
   - Can be used to regenerate HTML from markdown

## 📊 What's Inside

### The 9 Building Blocks

The guide covers all 9 components of the Business Model Canvas:

1. **Customer Segments** - Who you serve
2. **Value Propositions** - Why they should choose you
3. **Channels** - How you reach customers
4. **Customer Relationships** - How you build loyalty
5. **Revenue Streams** - How you make money
6. **Key Resources** - What you need to operate
7. **Key Activities** - What you do daily
8. **Key Partnerships** - Who you work with
9. **Cost Structure** - What it costs to operate

### Sample Business Plan: GreenBowl

A complete worked example of a sustainable meal delivery service including:
- Customer segments and market validation
- Value propositions and differentiation
- Multi-channel distribution strategy
- Revenue model with pricing
- Financial projections
- Unit economics and break-even analysis
- Visual business model diagram

The Business Model Canvas is meant to evolve:

1. **Draft** your initial model
2. **Validate** assumptions with customers
3. **Test** your riskiest assumptions first
4. **Iterate** based on feedback
5. **Refine** as you learn
6. **Optimize** for profitability and growth
